Co-Founder

A few friends and I wanted a way to slow down the fastest 4 years of our life, college, and give ourselves a way to reminisce on the good times, once we were out of them. To do just that, we create Sozu, a video journaling application that modernizes memory keeping and promotes introspection and self awareness.

Sozu allows users to track personal growth and mood trends by creating daily video entries on the app. As a co-founder I helped the designers and developers through the various ups and downs of a startup.

After pitching Sozu at Start-up Grind’s pitch competition in Chicago, IL, we won second place and had the opportunity to speak with various VC’s about the future of our brand.
