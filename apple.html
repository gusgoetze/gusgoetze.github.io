My Philosophy

I believe that we have a moral obligation to express our creativity and passions to the maximum. As social creatures, we are meant to interact with one another and share our crafts. As time moves incessantly, maximizing each moment on your timeline of life is crucial. The idea of time ticking away, wiping away seconds off my life with every breath I take, is not be a discouraging thought, but rather one of great motivation. With limited opportunities and finite time, it is my mission to share my craft, my creativity and my craziness with the world.
