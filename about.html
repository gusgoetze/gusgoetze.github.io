My story

I started out as a video creative in Chicago, Illinois. I was on a mission to tell the stories of others through my lens and capture the world's beauty on film. My passion for digital media was fueled by my peers, whom urged me to pursue a career in the film industry until one day, I pivoted.


I grew tired of the nepotistic bureaucracy that is the backbone of entertainment. Having been in close proximity with paramount technology, the finest cameras, computers and software, I always had a love for technology brewing inside me. Now based in Los Angeles, California as a student at the University of Southern California, I learned it wasn't film that I was passionate about, rather film was simply the means through which I was expressing my creative energy. Now, I am channeling that same creative ambition through technology, in pursuit of success and happiness as a tech industry professional.
