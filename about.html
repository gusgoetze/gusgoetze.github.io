My story

I started out as a video creative in Chicago, Illinois. I was on a mission to tell the stories of others through my lens and capture the world's beauty on film. My passion for digital media was fueled by my peers, who urged me to pursue a career in the film industry until one day, I pivoted.


I grew tired of the nepotistic bureaucracy that is the backbone of entertainment. Having been in close proximity with paramount technology, with the finest cameras, computers and software, I always had a love for technology brewing inside myself. Now based in Los Angeles, California as a student at the University of Southern California, I learned it wasn't film that I was passionate about, rather film was simply the means through which I was expressing my creative energy. Now, I am channeling that same creative ambition through technology, in pursuit of success and happiness as a tech industry professional.

My Philosophy

I believe that we have a moral obligation to express our creativity and passions to the maximum. As social creatures, we are meant to interact with one another and share our crafts. As time moves incessantly, maximizing each moment on your timeline of life is crucial. The idea of time ticking away, wiping away seconds off my life with every breath I take, is not be a discouraging thought, but rather one of great motivation. With limited opportunities and finite time, it is my mission to share my craft, my creativity and my craziness with the world.
